  • Shashkov, T. (2024). Ruin Probability Approximation for Bidimensional Brownian Risk Model with Tax

    Abstract: In this paper we derive the exact asymptotics of the ruin probability in the Brownian risk model with tax. We show that the ruin probability decays exponentially with rate given by the solution of an optimization problem. We also derive the asymptotics of the ruin time and the conditional distribution of the ruin time given ruin occurs.
